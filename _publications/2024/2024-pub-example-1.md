abstract: >-
  This work presents a Federated Learning framework for diagnosing brain tumors while preserving patient data privacy through secure model parameter sharing facilitated by Blockchain technology. By using an accelerated weighted average aggregation method, the framework achieved impressive accuracy in classifying four tumor types, showcasing its efficiency and potential for collaborative medical diagnosis.
